double Continent::getEarthRadius ( const double  lat) const

Get radius (in km) of WGS84 ellipsoid at a given latitude.

Compute value of WGS84 ellipsoid at a given theta.

Assuming that the Earth is a perfect ellipsoid, use the WGS84 major and minor radii to compute the WGS84 ellipsoid radius at a given latitude

Uses $R = {ab}{sqrt{a^2 ^2() + b^2 ^2()}}$ with a given by WGS84 and b = (1 - f)*a with f given by WGS84
